Biography

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is a former international cricketer from India, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He was born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, India.

Achievements

Cricket Records

Sachin Tendulkar holds numerous records, including being the highest run-score in both Test and One-Day International (ODI) cricket. He achieved several milestones throughout his illustrious career.

International Honors

He received several awards and honors, including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civillian award, in 2014. His contribution to Indian cricket is unparalleled.

Records

Sachin Tendulkar holds records for the most runs in international cricket, most centuries in both Test and ODI formats, and may more. His records are a testament to his exceptional skill and longevity in the sport.
